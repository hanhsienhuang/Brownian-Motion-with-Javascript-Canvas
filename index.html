Browian Motion

This is a visualization of the the equations $$ \left\{ \begin{aligned} \mathbf{x}_{t+1} &= \mathbf{x}_t + \mathbf{v}_t \\ \mathbf{v}_{t+1} &= (1-\lambda)\mathbf{v}_t + \alpha\mathbf{\eta} \end{aligned} \right. $$ where \(\lambda\) and \(\alpha\) are coefficients and \(\eta\) is Gaussian noise with std = 1.

01

\(\alpha = \)
